# KRC Woollens

## What is this site?
This is a React and Vite based frontend website for KRC Woollens. It serves as a modern, responsive e-commerce mockup, featuring multiple views including Home, Products, Category listings, Product Details, and more.

## Purpose
The primary purpose of this website is to showcase the catalog of KRC Woollens and provide a seamless digital shopping experience. It allows visitors to explore various categories such as Outerwear, Knitwear, Shawls, Tweed, and Cosmetics, while learning about the rich heritage and story behind each product.

## About KRC
KRC (Kumaon Regimental Centre) Woollens offers premium handcrafted woollens and natural products meticulously crafted by the brave women of our martyred soldiers, supported by the Veer Nari programme. Each piece, from fine tweeds and outerwear to pure Himalayan cosmetics, embodies tradition, warmth, and the enduring strength of the artisans who create them. Their craft represents a proud military legacy interwoven with local Himalayan heritage.
